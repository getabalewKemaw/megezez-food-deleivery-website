Welcome to MEGEZEZ

Where Ethiopian Tradition Meets Culinary Innovation

Established in Debre Berhan university, MEGEZEZ has been celebrating Ethiopia's rich culinary heritage since 2024. Our passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality creates unforgettable dining experiences.

Using generations-old recipes combined with modern techniques, our master chefs craft dishes that tell stories of Ethiopia's diverse regions. From our signature injera made with ancient grains to our specialty coffee ceremony, every element is a tribute to Ethiopian culture.

We source ingredients locally, supporting regional farmers and ensuring the freshest flavors. Our commitment to sustainability extends from our kitchen practices to our eco-friendly packaging.

FAQ

Do you offer vegetarian/vegan options?

+

Yes! We have dedicated vegetarian and vegan menu sections, with 15+ plant-based traditional and modern dishes. All vegan items are prepared separately to avoid cross-contamination.

What are your opening hours?

+

We're open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary - check our social media for special announcements.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major payment options in bank : mobile payments ( TeleBirr), and bank transfers. All online transactions are securely processed through Dashen Bank's, Nib International BanK, CBE,Abisinia Bank,Telebirr payment gateway.

Can I book for large groups or events?

+

We accommodate groups up to 20 people. For events larger than 15 guests, please contact our events team at events@megezez.com at least 72 hours in advance. We offer special menus for special events

What areas do you deliver to?

+

We deliver in Debre Berhan university center through our partner delivery services. Delivery hours: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily. Minimum order: 20 ETB for delivery.

Are your ingredients locally sourced?

+

85% of our ingredients come from local farmers and producers within Amhara Region. We work directly with 12 local cooperatives and use 100% organic spices .

How do you handle food allergies?

+

Notify our staff about any allergies before ordering. We maintain separate preparation areas for common allergens (nuts, gluten, shellfish). Allergen information available on request - ask for our allergy matrix menu.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

+

We follow all MOH guidelines: daily staff temperature checks, UV sanitized tableware, HEPA air filtration, and contactless payment options. Dining tables are spaced 2 meters apart, with optional outdoor seating.
